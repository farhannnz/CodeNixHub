# Web3Forms - 2 Minute Setup

## Step 1: Get Your Access Key (FREE)
1. Go to **web3forms.com**
2. Enter your email: `codenixhubtechnologies@gmail.com`
3. Click "Create Access Key"
4. Copy the access key (looks like: `abc123-def456-ghi789`)

## Step 2: Update Your Code
1. Open `js/contact-form.js`
2. Find line 67: `formData.append('access_key', '8b5a02c8-7c4d-4f5e-9a3b-1e2f3d4c5b6a');`
3. Replace the key with your real key

## That's it! 

Your form will now send emails directly to `codenixhubtechnologies@gmail.com`

## Test it:
1. Fill your contact form
2. Click send
3. Check your email inbox
4. You should get a formatted email with all the form data
